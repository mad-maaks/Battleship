    How to Play

  • Start by placing your ships on your board
  • Right-click to rotate ships during placement
  • Ships cannot touch each other (including diagonally)
  • Once both players have placed their ships, take turns attacking
  • Red marks indicate hits, blue marks indicate misses
  • Sink all enemy ships to win!
